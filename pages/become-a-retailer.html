Frequently asked

Common questions from new retailers

Do I need to commit to minimum quantities?

There's an opening order to make sure your store is properly stocked from day one. The right size depends on your floor space, customer traffic and product mix — we'll work that out together on the discovery call.

Are territories exclusive?

Not by default. We're mindful about retailer density so partners aren't competing on the same street, but exclusivity isn't a standard part of our trade program. If it's important to your model, raise it on the call and we'll discuss what's possible.

Do you provide working display units?

Working displays are the single best driver of in-store conversion, and we know it. Whether one makes sense for your store depends on the space, your customer base and your stocking commitment — happy to talk it through.

What about installation referrals?

We're rolling out customer-facing installation bookings through our website — your end customers can arrange a qualified plumber directly, so it stays off your plate and ensures the tap is installed correctly.

How does warranty support work?

Customers register their tap with us at point of purchase and contact us directly for any warranty matters — your team isn't stuck mediating between us and the customer.

I'm not a kitchen showroom — can I still apply?

Absolutely. We work with appliance retailers, builders, designers, specifiers and plumbing merchants. The application form has a "business type" question — pick the closest fit and tell us more in the message field.
