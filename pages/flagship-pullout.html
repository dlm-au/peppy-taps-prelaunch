Pull-Out Flexibility, Streamlined Form

The retractable hose extends your reach for filling pots, rinsing produce, and cleaning the sink — paired with intuitive lever operation for everyday simplicity. Four functions, one elegant fixture.

Boiling, Ambient, Hot & Cold

Filtered boiling at 98°C for instant tea and cooking, filtered ambient for clean everyday drinking water, plus your standard hot and cold mains — covered in a single fixture.
