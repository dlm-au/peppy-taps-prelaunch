About Peppy Taps

Convenience, sustainability and aesthetics — all in one tap.

Filtered boiling at 98°C, optional filtered chilled at 4–14°C or filtered ambient, with hot & cold mains — Peppy Taps brings everything you need in the kitchen to a single, beautifully designed fixture.

Why Peppy Taps

A better tap for the modern kitchen

Peppy Taps brings convenience, sustainability and aesthetics all in one tap. The ability to have filtered 98° water and filtered chilled water on demand — with the ultra-safe spring-loaded hot water handle ready to protect loved ones.

Available in five premium finishes — Chrome, Matte Black, Brushed Nickel, Gun Metal Grey and Brushed Brass Gold — there's a Peppy Tap for every kitchen style.

What we stand for

Four pillars, one tap

Every Peppy Tap is engineered to deliver on four things at once.

Smart technology

Touch-control panel and eight adjustable boiling temperatures (75°–98°C) in our Signature Collection.

Style

Slimline design with five premium finishes that adds elegant restraint to any kitchen.

Safety first

Spring-loaded handle, intentional design that prevents accidental activation, and reduced pressure to prevent splash-back.

Sustainability

Built to last, reduces single-use plastic, and saves the energy wasted on heating water you'll never use.

Safety

Designed for peace of mind

The tap supplies 98° water instantly — and we've made safety a priority in its design. The tap is carefully engineered so it can't be knocked or turned on unintentionally, and the water pressure is reduced to ensure no splash-back occurs.

That ultra-safe spring-loaded hot-water handle is always ready to protect loved ones, especially around children and busy kitchens.

Sustainability

A product that lasts — and saves

Peppy Taps reduces waste, saves energy, and saves money. Here's how.

Energy efficiency

  • Near-boiling 98° water on demand — no kettle required
  • Heated water is stored in a stainless steel 2.4 L tank, enough for 10 cups of tea or coffee
  • Filtered boiling and filtered chilled on demand saves time
  • No energy wasted heating water you'll never drink

Waste reduction

  • Reduces waste water
  • Reduces plastic consumption — no need for bottled water
  • Reduces energy waste

Cost savings

  • Saves money on energy-intensive appliances used multiple times a day
  • Saves time — a kettle takes ~3 minutes to boil; over 5 boils a day, that's 15 minutes back to you
  • Long product life means lower replacement cost
