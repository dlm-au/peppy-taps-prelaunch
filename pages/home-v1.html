Our Story

Where Great Design
Meets Everyday Ease

Peppy Taps was founded on a simple belief: that convenience, sustainability and aesthetics should come together in one tap. We design multi-function kitchen taps that deliver filtered boiling water at 98°C, filtered chilled or ambient water, and standard hot & cold mains — all through a single, elegantly designed fixture.

The Signature Collection features touch-control technology with an integrated digital panel — including a 5-in-1 model that adds chilled sparkling water on demand. The Flagship Collection offers the same engineered reliability in a cleaner, lever-controlled form. Every tap is available in five premium finishes.

Designed and supported in Australia, every Peppy Tap is built around the small details that make daily kitchen tasks effortless — and backed by a long-term commitment to quality, replaceable parts, and the people who choose them.
